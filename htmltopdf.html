Produt Data Sheet

DEO FORCE 15w40

Grades: MP‐2 & MP‐3

Description

EURON PLUS 15W-40 is a multi-purpose lubricant oil for Pickups, Minibuses, Commercial vehicles, Light and Heavy Duty engines

Application

EURON PLUS DEO FORCE 15W-40 is a next-level diesel engine oil formed to deliver excellent safeguard for task force containing light pickup vehicles, minibuses, LCV’s and heavy duty engines from a variety of manufacturers. Suitable as a lubricant in case of fleet composed of new & old generation engines on light pickup vehicles, Minibuses, Commercial vehicles, Light and Heavy Duty engines thus allowing the run-of-the-mill of lubricants to be reduced.

Performance

API - CI-4 / SL Performance

Benefits

  • Protective shield and endurance of multi-fleet engines.
  • Better-quality dispersant and anti-oxidation properties for better safety against smoke build-up and oil filter clogging.
  • Numerous Anti-Wear properties for bore polishing protection.
  • Advance detergent properties for piston cleanliness and piston groove deposits control.
  • Anti-corrosion, Anti-rust, Anti-foam.
  • High total base number (TBN) for extended oil drain interval.
